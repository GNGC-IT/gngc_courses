**Focus:** Circuit design, soldering, and cable management.

- **Workshop Sessions:**
    
    - **Power Distribution:** Identifying the voltage and current needs of sensors vs. actuators. Implementing voltage regulators or separate power rails if necessary.
        
    - **Prototyping to Permanent:** Moving from breadboards to soldered protoboards or neatly secured looms.
        
    - **Cable Management:** Applying the "clean cable management" requirement from the rubric using cable ties, looms, or 3D-printed clips.
        
- **Documentation Requirement:** * A formal circuit diagram (using Fritzing or Tinkercad).
    
    - Safety checklist (checking for short circuits and correct resistor values).
